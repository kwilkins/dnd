Character Ancestries - The stuff you were born and raised with

Aasimar

Aasimar are placed in the world to serve as guardians of law and good. Their patrons expect them to strike at evil, lead by example, and further the cause of justice.


  • Traits: +2 Charisma, Darkvision, Celestial Resistance, Healing Hands, Light Bearer
  • Subrace Variety: Choice between different subraces that offer additional traits

    • Probably a: Bard, Paladin, Sorcerer, or Warlock
    Source: PHB

    Dragonborn

    Dragonborn look very much like dragons standing upright in two legs, though they lack wings or a tail.


  • Traits: +2 Strength, +1 Charisma, Draconic Ancestry, Breath Weapon, Damage Resistance.

    • Probably a: Barbarian, Fighter, or Paladin
    Source: PHB

    Dwarf

    Bold and hardy, dwarves are known as skilled warriors, miners, and workers of stone and metal. Scottish accent not required.


  • Traits: +2 Constitution, Darkvision, Dwarven Resilience, Dwarven Combat Training, Stonecunning
  • Subrace Variety: Choice between different subraces that offer additional traits

    • Probably a: Barbarian, Cleric, or Fighter
    Source: PHB

    Elf

    Elves are a magical people of otherworldly grace, living in the world but not entirely part of it.


  • Traits: +2 Dexterity, Darkvision, Keen Senses, Fey Ancestry, Trance
  • Subrace Variety: Choice between different subraces that offer additional traits

    • Probably a: Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue, or Wizard
    Source: PHB

    Firbolg

    Firbolg tribes cloister in remote forest strongholds, preferring to spend their days in quiet harmony with the woods.


  • Traits: +2 Wisdom, +1 Strength, Firbolg Magic, Hidden Step, Powerful Build, Speech of Beast and Leaf

    • Probably a: Cleric, Barbarian, Monk, or Ranger
    Source: PHB

    Gnome

    A gnome's energy and enthusiasm for living shines through every inch of their tiny body.


  • Traits: +2 Intelligence, Darkvision, Gnome Cunning
  • Subrace Variety: Choice between different subraces that offer additional traits

    • Probably a: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, or Wizard
    Source: PHB

    Goliath

    Strong and reclusive, every day brings a new challenge to a goliath.


  • Traits: +2 Strength, +1 Constitution, Natural Athlete, Stone's Endurance, Powerful Build, Mountain Born

    • Probably a: Barbarian, Fighter, or Paladin
    Source: PHB

    Half-Elf

    Half-elves combine what some say are the best qualities of their elf and human parents.


  • Traits: +2 Charisma, +1 to Two Other Ability Scores, Darkvision, Fey Ancestry, Skill Versatility

    • Probably a: Bard, Paladin, Sorcerer, or Warlock
    Source: PHB

    Halfling

    The diminutive halflings survive in a world full of larger creatures by avoiding notice or, barring that, avoiding offense.


  • Traits: +2 Dexterity, Lucky, Brave, Halfling Nimbleness
  • Subrace Variety: Choice between different subraces that offer additional traits

    • Probably a: Bard, Monk, Rogue, Sorcerer or Warlock!
    Source: PHB

    Half-Orc

    Half-orcs' duller pigmentation, sloping foreheads, jutting jaws, prominent teeth, and towering builds make their orcish heritage plain for all to see.


  • Traits: +2 Strength, +1 Constitution, Darkvision, Menacing, Relentless Endurance, Savage Attacks

    • Probably a: Barbarian, Fighter, or Paladin
    Source: PHB

    Human

    Humans are the most adaptable among the common ancestries. Whatever drives them, humans are the innovators, the achievers, and the pioneers of the worlds.


  • Traits: +2 and +1 to Ability Scores of your choice, Extra Language and Toolset, Improvise

    • Equally good with all classes
    Source: PHB

    Tiefling

    Tieflings' infernal heritage has left a clear imprint on their appearance, giving them horns, tails, and a range of skin tones. They commonly are greeted with stares, whispers, and fear.

  • Traits: +2 Charisma, Darkvision, Hellish Resistance, Infernal Legacy
  • Subrace Variety: Choice between different subraces that offer additional traits

    • Probably a: Bard, Paladin, Sorcerer, or Warlock
    Source: PHB
